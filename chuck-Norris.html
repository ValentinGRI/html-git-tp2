About Chuck Norris

Chuck Norris is a legendary martial artist, actor, film producer, and screenwriter. He is best known for his roles in action films and television series, especially "Walker, Texas Ranger."

Chuck Norris is not just an icon in the entertainment industry; he is also a skilled martial artist with black belts in Tang Soo Do, Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu, and Judo.
